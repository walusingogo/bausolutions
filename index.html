Where automation helps

The biggest delays are usually small process failures.

Most operational drag comes from repeat work with no clear owner, no single status, and too many manual steps between teams.

Approvals with no owner

Requests sit in inboxes, approvals stall, and nobody can see where the delay is.

  • Approval routing by role or value
  • Escalations when deadlines are missed
  • One visible status from start to finish

The same data entered twice

Teams capture information in one place, then retype it elsewhere later.

  • Structured digital forms
  • Cleaner records and fewer mistakes
  • System-to-system handoffs where possible

No clear audit trail

Work gets done, but the business cannot easily prove who approved what and when.

  • Time-stamped workflow history
  • Decision and exception tracking
  • Better reporting for management and compliance
