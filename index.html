Industrial Services

At BAU Solutions, we offer a comprehensive range of Industrial Services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Below is an overview of the services we provide:

We specialize in the overhaul of final drives for loaders and personnel carriers, ensuring your equipment operates at peak performance. Our maintenance programs are tailored to extend the life of your machinery and reduce downtime.

Our cylinder servicing includes inspection, repair, and refurbishment of hydraulic and pneumatic cylinders to ensure optimal functionality and safety in your operations.

We manufacture high-quality, custom hoses to meet specific requirements, ensuring durability and reliability in various industrial applications.

We offer expert servicing for conveyor systems and crushers, including installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting to ensure smooth operations and minimal downtime.

Our team provides comprehensive servicing for centrifugal multistage pumps, focusing on performance optimization and efficiency improvements.

We provide maintenance and repair services for rock breakers, ensuring they operate efficiently and effectively in demanding environments.

Our experienced technicians offer field support to assist with on-site repairs and maintenance, ensuring your operations run smoothly, no matter where you are.

We manage projects from inception to completion, providing expert draughting services to ensure your projects are executed on time and within budget.

Our multidisciplinary team is equipped to handle a wide range of engineering services, including civil, structural, electrical, and mechanical work, tailored to your project needs.

We provide advanced filtration systems to enhance the quality and reliability of fuels and lubricants, reducing contamination and improving operational efficiency.

Our expert team ensures thorough spillage clean-up, restoring affected areas and minimizing environmental impact.

We offer advanced fuel management and maintenance systems to optimize fuel usage, monitor consumption, and enhance overall efficiency.

Our team ensures accurate calibration of fluid control meters, maintaining precision in measurement and enhancing system reliability.

We specialize in installing and commissioning lube equipment to meet the specific requirements of industrial operations.

Our depot maintenance and tank cleaning services ensure safe and efficient storage solutions for fuels and lubricants.

We provide end-to-end solutions for lubrication and lube workshop set-ups, tailored to meet the needs of diverse industries.

We specialize in overhauling heavy equipment machinery to ensure reliability and enhanced performance in demanding environments.

We supply high-quality spares for underground loaders, ensuring seamless operations and minimizing downtime.
